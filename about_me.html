About Me

Hello and Welcome to my Personal Website!

I am a Software Engineer at ERP Suites and a graduate of Purdue University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. Previously, I was an Undergraduate Researcher for the Artificial Intelligence for Musicians (AIM) team, where I contributed to the development of the Companion App, and an Artificial Intelligence Intern at ERP Suites. My primary interests lie in Generative AI, front-end development, and hardware design, where I aim to bridge the gap between intelligent software solutions and cutting-edge hardware systems. I am always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and discussions so please feel free to connect with me via email or through my social media platforms.
